Question: In a bustling city in Australia, the Clean Skies Construction (CSC) company was involved in building a new skyscraper. However, during the inspection, Alex noticed that the Safety Protocol Signage was outdated and did not comply with the recent updates to the Safety Regulations, specifically Clause 18 of the Work Health and Safety Regulations 2022 (Qld), which mandates current and clear safety instructional signage. Alex notified the site manager, but continued the inspection without waiting for immediate rectification. As Alex climbed an unsecured scaffold, a violation of Clause 24 regarding secure scaffolding, a mishap occurred although no injury was sustained. answer: The outdated safety signage and unsecured scaffolding.
