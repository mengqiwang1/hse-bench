Question: In a bustling factory, John, a safety officer, observed a worker using a grinding machine without wearing eye protection, while others around were complying with PPE policies. How should John apply the PPE policy if he notices non-compliance?
Option:
A. Ignore it and focus on other tasks
B. Report it to management
C. Immediately provide eye protection and training
D. Wait for the next safety audit
answer: C

Question: Sarah was responsible for conducting a safety audit at a manufacturing plant but noticed that several fire extinguishers were either missing or expired. What immediate action should Sarah take regarding the expired or missing extinguishers?
Option:
A. Ignore the issue for now
B. Request replacements and log the issue
C. Conduct a fire drill to test response
D. Move fire extinguishers from other areas
answer: B

Question: During a routine inspection, a construction site supervisor, Mike, noticed that workers were not following proper fall protection measures at elevated work areas. What should Mike prioritize to enhance on-site safety regarding falls?
Option:
A. Host a safety meeting
B. Remind workers verbally
C. Ensure strict enforcement of gear use

Question: During a chemical handling operation in a laboratory, Emma realized that the Safety Data Sheets (SDS) were not accessible to the workers handling hazardous substances. What should Emma do to address the problem of inaccessible Safety Data Sheets?

A. Notify OSHA
