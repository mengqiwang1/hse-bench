Question: In a bustling factory, John, a safety officer, observed a worker using a grinding machine without wearing eye protection, while others around were complying with PPE policies. What should be the outcome if PPE compliance is improved?
Option:
A. Increased productivity
B. Reduced accidents
C. Higher insurance costs
D. Non-compliance reports
answer: B

Question: Sarah was responsible for conducting a safety audit at a manufacturing plant but noticed that several fire extinguishers were either missing or expired. What is the likely outcome of properly maintaining fire extinguishing systems?
Option:
A. Enhanced safety compliance levels
B. Higher risk of fire incidents
C. Reduced need for safety audits
D. Increased fire drills frequency
answer: A

Question: During a routine inspection, a construction site supervisor, Mike, noticed that workers were not following proper fall protection measures at elevated work areas. What should be the result of addressing the fall protection gaps?

A. Increased project insurance premiums
B. Higher worker injury rates

answer: C

Question: During a chemical handling operation in a laboratory, Emma realized that the Safety Data Sheets (SDS) were not accessible to the workers handling hazardous substances. What should be the result of making SDS readily available?
Option:
A. Increased confusion among workers
B. Enhanced safety compliance
C. Higher risk of chemical exposure
